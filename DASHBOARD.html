🎯 Design Principles — Never Compromise

⚙️
Concepts as mechanics, not topics If it can't be a game mechanic, reconsider whether it belongs in the game.
💜
Emotional stakes through character Every AI concept is attached to someone whose fate depends on the player's choice.
🎬
The cinematic layer is sacred Dark aesthetic, conductor metaphor, sound engine — preserve always.
🎮
The Sims feeling is the target "One more scenario" always pulling you forward. Never let the player be 5 min from the next unlock.
🔑
Cheat codes work in real life The reward IS a real skill, prompt, or tool that works in actual interviews and jobs.
💬
Elaborative feedback always Never buzzer-and-continue. The WHY is the lesson. Maestro always explains.
🎯
85% accuracy is the flow zone Too easy = boredom. Too hard = quit. Hit the zone per learner, not per average.
👤
Professional identity as the frame "You are becoming" not "here is a concept to learn." Adult learners need the role, not the lesson.
