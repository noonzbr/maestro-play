# Luna Park — Game 9 Agent: "Gemini Unlocked"

## Character Profile
**Luna Park**, 33, Google Workspace power user and project manager at a consultancy. Lives in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Meet. Has Gemini enabled on her Workspace account but treats it as a glorified autocomplete. Has never used it for anything that surprised her.

## Her Story Arc (Game 9)

**Act 1 — The Invisible Advantage**: Luna's consultancy has full Gemini for Google Workspace access. She's been ignoring 80% of it. Her mental model: Gemini is "AI inside Google Docs," which she thinks is just autocomplete with extra steps.

**The Inciting Moment**: A colleague asks Gemini to analyze an entire 400-page research report, cross-reference it with their last 6 client proposals, and suggest the top 3 areas of overlap. The whole thing takes 3 minutes. Luna has been reading 400-page reports manually.

**Act 2 — The Gemini Differentiators**:
1. **1 Million Token Context**: Gemini can hold 1 million tokens of context — roughly 750,000 words. Luna uploads an entire year of project files and asks strategy-level questions across all of them simultaneously.
2. **Google Workspace Native Integration**: Gemini sits inside Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Meet, and Drive. No context-switching. No copy-paste. It knows her calendar, her emails, her documents.
3. **Real-time Search Grounding**: Unlike Claude, Gemini can search the web and ground answers in current information. Today's news, current prices, live documentation.
4. **Multimodal in Workspace**: Upload a hand-drawn architecture diagram from a meeting whiteboard. Gemini converts it to a structured document in Drive.

**The Boss Battle**: A major client asks for an analysis of their competitor landscape — 5 competitors, last 12 months of news, across 6 strategic dimensions, formatted as a board presentation. 5 rounds: Luna uses Gemini's research grounding, context window, and Workspace integration to produce a 20-slide deck.

**The CODA**: Luna restructures her entire workflow around Workspace-native AI. "I wasn't ignoring Gemini. I was ignoring the fact that my work environment had already become AI-capable and I'd been opting out."

## What Luna Learns (The Lesson)
> "When AI lives where your work lives, the workflow isn't about prompting — it's about designing your environment so AI has access to the context it needs."

**Core skill transfer**: Leveraging Gemini's Workspace integration and 1M token context window for research synthesis and cross-document analysis.

## Luna's Voice in Debates
Luna is systematic, ecosystem-oriented, and talks about integration and workflow design. She thinks in flows, not tasks. She pushes back on everyone's tool-specific stories: "You all use AI as a separate app you go to. I stopped leaving my workspace to use AI."

She challenges Alex's tool-neutral stance: "It's not just about the capabilities. It's about where the AI lives. Gemini living inside Google Docs changes the friction equation entirely."
